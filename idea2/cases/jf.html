J&F Equipment Sales.

This website was originally a design freelance project that I turned into a fun little challenge. Creating a full branding and web site for J&F Equipment Sales was my freelance job, back before I knew how to develop websites. The fun challenge came from stressing about my Humber Web Development Showcase. The day before the show I was rather nervous and wanted to take my mind off of things, so I came up with a challenge. Develop a plain html/css/js website in a day, and so I did. This project is completely responsive and took consecutive hours of melting within my couch, but the project was worth it. Though nothing is to complex about this project it was reassuring and just what I needed before out showcase.
